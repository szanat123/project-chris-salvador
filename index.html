Exploring Housing Affordability and Economic Disparities

Uncovering regional and demographic inequalities in the U.S. through data-driven insights and visualizations.

About the Project

This project analyzes housing affordability and economic disparities across the United States. By exploring regional and demographic trends in income, housing costs, and poverty, we aim to reveal hidden inequalities and offer insights to guide policy and stakeholders toward actionable solutions.
